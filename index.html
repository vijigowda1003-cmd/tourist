Indonesia is the world's largest archipelagic state, comprising over 17,000 islands between Southeast Asia and Australia. It is the fourth most populous country, with a rich, diverse culture, and features significant biodiversity, active volcanoes, and a major tropical rainforest. Jakarta is the current capital, situated on the main island of Java.
